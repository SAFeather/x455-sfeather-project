ABOUT

History

The Codman Swim Team has a rich and storied history that dates back to the early 1960s when a group of passionate swimmers came together to form a community dedicated to the sport. Originally known as the "Codman Aquatic Club," the team started with just a handful of members practicing in local lakes. Throughout the decades, the Codman Swim Team grew in both size and reputation, becoming a powerhouse in the region's swimming scene. With a focus on fostering talent, teamwork, and sportsmanship, the team attracted swimmers of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to seasoned competitors. In the 1980s, the Codman Swim Team expanded its facilities with the construction of the Codman Swimming Pool, complete with a brand new pool. This marked a new era for the team, allowing them to host prestigious competitions and attract top-tier coaches and athletes. Here is the first and only time we've won the Minuteman League Championships!

Beyond the pool, the team has also been active in community outreach, promoting water safety education and providing opportunities for underprivileged youth to learn to swim. Today, the Codman Swim Team continues to uphold its legacy of excellence, camaraderie, and dedication to the sport of swimming. With a strong foundation built on passion, perseverance, and teamwork, the team looks forward to creating even more waves of success in the years to come. This team is full of love and joy and is the absolute best part of any summer!

Coaches

The Codman Swim Team is known for its dedicated head coach Sarah Feather. Sarah has been working with the team for 5 years, as well as being a librarian in the town.

Other coaches include Jenny, Nick, Will, Casey, Tamara, and Liam. Codman coaches are good friends, as well as co-workers, and spend a lot of time in the summer together. It's a fun, but challenging, job that requires a good deal of commitment. Our coaches go through rigorous training and have been Codman lifeguards for years!
